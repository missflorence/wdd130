Bundles of Energy

Unlike their older counterparts, kittens are less than sure of their footing in the world. Most kittens aren't even half a year old, and that is why the focus on their small size is the key, as they can sneak around in corners one wouldn't suspect. For now, until they turn into adult cats, they have this playful energy, depending on their mood, of course. These kittens learn through play more than anything, and at this age, demand quite a lot of attention.

They are almost like human toddlers in a way, where they are able to process information and take it into their own hands. These kitten pictures may fit well into either two categories; one, where these kittens are in a playful mood, or two, when they are in a more calmer mood that they usually are. It's never easy to photograph a fidgety kitten, but in the right conditions, their human companions are able to photograph them just in the right moment; just before they are about to play!
